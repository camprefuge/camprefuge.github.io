Do You Need to Recharge?

The Soul’s Cry for Renewal

Marriage is tough. We pray you learned a lot your first time here at Camp Refuge. But we know from firsthand experience that if you aren’t working on your marriage, you’re sliding downhill. Allow us to handle all the details, so you can focus on growing even closer to the one God gave you as a partner in this life.

Building on the themes from the first retreat – communicating effectively, understanding our differences, seeking & granting forgiveness, and exercising the power of prayer - we will offer spirit-led content to facilitate greater levels of authenticity and transparency about important topics in your relationship.

The ‘safe haven’ you experienced before will remain; God is on these grounds, and He knows better than anyone what you need. Permit Him to do all that He wants to do in your lives and give Him all the glory for the stories on the other side!

We’re grateful you’ve trusted us before, and we’d be honored to again hold the space for you to experience God’s best for your relationship in the years to come. We hope to see you again soon!
