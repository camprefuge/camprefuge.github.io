Events

October 25-27, 2019, or
November 1-3, 2019

Rustic outdoors meets airy romance in this weekend retreat focused on building closer relationships between husbands and wives. It’s glamping at its finest with gourmet meals, bonfires, and times of intimate conversation without the distractions of everyday responsibilities. Topics include communication, the art of grace, and couple’s teambuilding. To get the most out of this weekend, phones and other electronic devices should be left behind or turned off.  Space is limited, so signup today!

Cost: $275/couple includes all meals and sleeping accommodations. 
Location:  Camp Refuge's temporary headquarters in Lafayette, LA

A Weekend Away

What is Glamping?

Glamping is camping with all the luxuries and refinements of a fine hotel. Sleeping accomodations will be a spacious yurt-style tent with a queen sized air mattress and full linens. Just bring your favorite pillow!

All your meals will be prepared by award winning Chef Bennett Simmons.  You’re sure to be blown away by the flavorful cuisine and the romantic atmosphere in which it’s served.  

Your days will include times in sessions focused on navigating the art of relationships, in song services, in team building exercises, and more.

The entire goal of the event is to provide an atmosphere where you and your spouse can refresh and disconnect from the worries of everyday life and spend 48 hours focused on your relationship with one another,  The wooded property along the Vermilion River where the retreat is hosted is a perfect getaway for you and the one you love - won’t you join us?
