Camp Refuge is...

Insert simple mission statement here -

Holy Spirit driven. We believe that all lasting change comes from the inner workings of the Holy Spirit. Without Him, our work and striving are fruitless. We desire to create an environment that allows the Holy Spirit to move freely. A place where married couples can let their guard down and invest in each other. A place where parents and their teen can genuinely connect without distractions. A place where youth can have an intimate encounter with the Father.

A resource in all seasons of life. We seek to not only provide a safe haven, but also equip individuals with the tools to reconcile, strengthen, and establish relationships.

VISION

PROPOSED MASTER PLAN

GATHERING:
The retreat centre will be the main gathering space, currently designed to house 250 persons in chairs. The main portion of the space will be flexible enough to allow for various elements of our program: the retreats’ Christian worship services, teaching sessions, breakout discussions, and dining, the educational courses, church conferences, weddings/ receptions, and reunions. A full service kitchen and large outdoor porch area will be part of this structure as well. The pondside chapel will serve as a more intimate gathering space on the couples’ side of the facility. It will seat around 65 persons, and host the Christian worship services, teaching sessions, group sharing and ministry time for the married couples, pastors, and smaller-scale retreats. The outdoor amphitheatre will serve as the gathering place for bonfires, camp skits, storytelling, and an alternative location for worship services and teaching sessions. The cooking pavilion will serve as an alternative location for cooking, dining, group sharing and teaching during the retreats.

ACCOMODATIONS:
The 12 private treehouse cottages will surround the existing pond, connected via a raised wooden boardwalk. One will be accessible more easily from the camp drive. All will accommodate 2 persons with a bed, casual dining area, and full bathroom. These are intended to house the married couples for the husband/wife and pastor retreats. The men’s and women’s dormitories are intended to house 100 guests each for the youth, parent/teen, teambuilding, and church retreats, complete with bunkrooms and bathroom facilities. The bathouse is an independent structure intended for servicing all guests.

ADVENTURE:
The riverside boat launch will allow guests to experience canoeing adventures on the Vermilion River during their free time. A splash pad located near the dormitories will provide a much needed respite from the heat of Louisiana summers. The high and low ropes courses will strengthen and reinforce relationship principles taught throughout the retreats, and will be the principal activity of day team-building retreats.
