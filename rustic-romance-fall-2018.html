OCTOBER 26-28, 2018

Rustic outdoors meets airy romance in this weekend retreat focused on building closer relationships between husbands and wives. It’s glamping at its finest with gourmet meals, bonfires, and times of intimate conversation without the distractions of everyday responsibilities. Topics include communication, the art of grace, and couple’s teambuilding. To get the most out of this weekend, phones and other electronic devices should be left behind or turned off. SPECIAL RATES APPLY for this GRAND OPENING weekend and space is limited.  **Registration opens mid-late August. 

Introductory Price: $150/couple includes all meals and sleeping accommodations. 
Location:  Camp Refuge's’ temporary headquarters in Lafayette, LA
