August 2, 2025

Looking for a great way to start a life-long relationship? Our Firm Foundations retreat offers engaged couples - or those seriously dating - a day retreat to invest in your future marriage. Spend a day unplugged from the busyness of life and discover God’s heartbeat for your closest relationship. You will enjoy the beauty of our grounds while you get a fresh view on how to navigate the beauty and complexities of marriage. To get the most out of this retreat, phones and other electronic devices should be left behind or turned off. Space is limited, so signup today!

Cost: $150/couple - registration includes lunch, snacks, and drinks 
Location:  Camp Refuge - 1120 Tilac Road - Breaux Bridge, LA
