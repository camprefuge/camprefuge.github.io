HOST YOUR EVENT AT CAMP REFUGE

Camp Refuge is the perfect place to host your next gathering.

Need a venue to get away from it all? Situated on 42 secluded acres in beautiful Breaux Bridge, LA, Camp Refuge offers a safe haven like no other. We would be delighted to host you and your small group. Though daytime retreats are our specialty, overnight accommodations are available. Let us know how we can best accommodate you or your group. Facility tours are available by appointment.

Celebrate

Here in Cajun country, we know how to pass a good time!  Have a special occasion that you need a small venue for? Consider hosting at our small gathering house.  We can host family holiday gatherings, family or class reunions, birthday and graduation parties, bridal and baby showers, and more.  Intimate wedding ceremonies and receptions are also possible.  We have both indoor and outdoor options.

Retreat

Retreats are what we do best. Experience the peace and serenity the grounds have to offer, and you won’t want to leave. Our heart is to share this safe haven with you to afford your group the opportunity to reflect, grow, and heal. Host your church group retreats (men’s, women’s, marriage, small groups), leadership retreats (executive team, church staff, school faculty), and team retreats (corporate, sports, collegiate groups) in an unforgettable place.

Teambuild

We have a heart to coach teams to greater unity through challenging group activities. Bring your sports, leadership, academic, or corporate team for growth opportunities that will continue to impress truth upon you long after you leave the grounds. Teams don’t grow in their comfort zones, so expect to be challenged, stretched, and uncomfortable through our various ‘low ropes’ activities led by Camp Refuge staff.

Small Gathering

Situated on a 3-acre pond, this meeting space boasts a large front porch overlooking the water.  The interior open floor plan has an open flexible space, beautifully appointed with vaulted cathedral ceilings, You can customize these spaces in many different ways to meet your exact needs.

PreviousNext

Levi’s House

This 4 bedroom, 3 bath house can comfortably sleep 15 guests on beds. The master suite has a king-sized bed and separate tub/shower. The other three bedrooms have bunk beds and can sleep from 3 to 6 people each. One bedroom has a private bath. The open living, dining, kitchen is great for gatherings.

PreviousNext

Glamping Tents

Each of our twelve glamping tents offer a bit of rustic luxury. Outfitted with wither a queen air bed cot, or two twins, each tent also has furnishings to make your stay most comfortable. The raised wood deck and porch chairs completes your stay away from home for your retreat experience.

Adventure

Every retreat center rental gives you access to the 42 acres nestled in the wooded haven. From canoeing, to our 1-mile loop trail, to areas for archery, bonfires, and a prayer/meditation space we call “The Hollow”, there are so many site amenities to explore.
